🏯 ABOUT VIDYAMURA
⛩️
VidyaMura
Cultivate Your Knowledge in Harmony
Vidya (বিদ্যা) means knowledge in Sanskrit, and Mura (村) means village in Japanese. Together — a village built on knowledge. A place where every path walked, every task done, and every hour studied brings you closer to mastery.

🎯 The Mission
Most study apps track tasks. VidyaMura tracks you — your consistency, your growth, your journey. Built for competitive exam warriors who need more than a to-do list. Every streak, every hour, every path is a step toward your goal.
